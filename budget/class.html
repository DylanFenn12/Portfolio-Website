Category definitions

🏠

Housing & Living

Lounge rent and monthly housing corp payments, apartment rent, and parlor costs. Covers the physical house and its upkeep.

🎉

Social & Brotherhood

Mixers, date parties, formal, and brotherhood events. Discretionary social programming for the chapter.

🏛️

National & IFC Dues

Payments to Nationals and the IFC required to remain a chartered, in-good-standing chapter.

📣

Recruitment & Apparel

Rush events and materials, plus chapter apparel and gear.

❤️

Philanthropy & Perks

Philanthropy events and associated fundraising, plus shared amenities like streaming services and game-day subscriptions.

For exact figures, contact your class officers or the chapter treasurer.
